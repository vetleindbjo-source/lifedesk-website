What it does

One desk for everything you keep in your head.

Just write it

“brukte 200 på kiwi”, “ring tannlegen fredag”, “fikk 42000 i lønn” — it lands in the right bucket, list, or calendar. No forms.

A budget that holds

Necessities are funded first, then the rest splits your way. Rename the buckets — “Fuck you money” if you like.

It checks in

Quiet weekly and monthly summaries, waiting for you when you open it. Never a notification you didn't ask for.

Everywhere

Web, desktop, and iPhone. Your data follows your account, in Norwegian or English — switch any time.

The budget

Three buckets. Necessities first.

Log your income once and LifeDesk funds your fixed costs, then splits whatever's left between Fun and Savings on your terms. The bars show exactly where the month stands — at a glance, set like a ledger.

  • Fixed costs come off the top, automatically
  • A savings floor protects tight months
  • Rename the buckets to whatever fits your life
